Suman Saha

I am a PhD student at the Department of Computing and Communication Technologies in Oxford Brookes University. My Director of Studies and first supervisor is Professor Fabio Cuzzolin. Professor Nigel Crook and Dr Tjeerd Olde Scheper are my two other PhD supervisors. I am an active member of the Artificial Intelligence and Vision Research Group led by Professor Fabio Cuzzolin. I consider myself fortunate to have an opportunity to work closely with the world renowned Torr Vision Group (TVG) in the Department of Engineering Science at University of Oxford. More specifically, I work with my PhD guide Dr Michael Sapienza and Professor Philip H. S. Torr. who is the founder of TVG. My research areas are Computer Vision and Machine Learning and I am interested in applying Deep Learning techniques in solving challenging Computer Vision problems, for example, the spatio-temporal human action localisation problem in videos. We have recently proposed a novel Deep Learning based spatio-temporal action localisation framework which has shown superior performance and become the state-of-the-art.

Before coming to Oxford Brookes, I was an MSc student at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, University of Bedfordshire (UoB), United Kingdom. During my MSc, I worked in the Department of Computer Science, University of Oxford under the supervision of Dr Ashutosh Natraj and Sonia Waharte and proposed a novel realtime algorithm for frontal obstacle detection and avoidance for low cost unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). The related publication can be accessed using this link. At University of Bedfordshire, I was received an MSc in Computer Science with double Distinction (Scored 94.27% marks). I completed my undergraduate Polytechnic Engineering in Computer Science at Siddaganga Polytechnic College, Tumkur, Karnataka, India.

During the Summer of 2013, I worked as a research intern in a EPSRC funded project titled New Foundational Structures for Engineering Verified multi-UAVs" with Dr Ashutosh Natraj. Back in my home country India, I was a full-time employee at the the Research and Development and Scientific Services division of Tata Steel Limited at Jamshedpur. I worked there for six wonderful years (Nov. 2006 - Aug. 2012) as a Software Analyst under the supervision of DR. Sumitesh Das, Chief (Global Research Programmes) at Tata Steel Ltd. I started my professional career in July 2002 in SICT Pvt. Ltd. Jamshedpur as a Java and Web developer. I worked there till October 2006.
